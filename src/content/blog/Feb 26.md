I wanted to let everyone know that the new book is coming along, all be it, much slower than I originally thought. It is going to take several more months of work. I think there is going to be value in it, and I don't want to rush anything out with my books anymore. They take the time they take.
Thus, I won't be doing any interviews, or likely making any videos, for most of 2026. Actually video platforms I am finding very concerning now. A few years ago YT became the place where the most people went searching for information. A video became their go to source of anything over the written word. Thas has only increased, yet so too has AI. The majority of videos are now AI generated, or AI added, or AI recommended. Thumbnails have to shock everyone, and commment sections that are open just promote conflict and antagonism. 
We are no longer in the video era of 2010-15, a time of great wealth of open human information. It is changing, and changing fast, and I really don't know at this point whether I want to be a part of where it is all going or not. Now there are not even solid 10 minute clear detailed videos anymore. They tend to either be 1 minute shorts, or 2 hour long semi-rambles...its not a good switch.
I watch mostly nothing anymore, save a few people that I have followed for several years, and catch them for short updates on what the current sitation of reality is at. Beyond that, I stick to my work, and stillness. 
My focus for 2026 is going to be on the written word. I will keep going on this book until complete, take a short break, then get going on another that I had in mind last summer. What videos come during that time will be small additions to the writing.
I do have a few articles as well, or lets say things that could go into those books but with no space for them, I will add them here periodically. The first will be in early March on the Ken Grimwood novel Replay, and a few insights I got after looking into it again last month.
Wishing you well. When the book is finally available I will let you.
